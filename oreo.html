2 Ingredient Oreo Mug Cake
By: Crazy for Crust

Ingredients

4 Oreos
3 Tablespoons Milk

Instructions

1. Add oreos (4 regular sized or 16 mini) to mug and crush them
2. Add milk (3 tbsp)
3. Microwave for 1 minute (or less depending on your preference)

Tips!

1. Any kind of Oreos will work!
2. If you don't like chunks in your cake make sure to mash up the Oreos as much as possible.
