Sugar Cookie in a Mug
By: Kathleen's Cravings

Ingredients

1 Tablespoon Butter
2 Tablespoons White Granulated Sugar
Pinch of Salt (Omit if Using Salted Butter)
3 Tablespoons Milk
1 Egg Yolk
¬º Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
3 Tablespoons All-Purpose Flour
Sprinkles, Measure with Your ‚ù§Ô∏è (optional)

Instructions

1. Melt butter (1 tbsp.) for 30 sec.
2. Add sugar (2 tbsp.), egg yolk, and vanilla (1/4 tsp.). Stir in flour (3 tbsp.), salt (pinch), and optional sprinkles!
3. Microwave for 1 minute (or less depending on your preference)

Tips!

I wouldn't worry about measuring the vanilla especially if you love it like me!
