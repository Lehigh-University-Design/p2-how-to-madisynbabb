About

Hi! My name is Madisyn and I’m currently a sophomore at Lehigh University studying marketing and graphic design.

As a lazy college student myself who loves baking I wanted to create a How-to Guide on how to make your life easier- just in case those dining hall desserts aren’t cutting it for you.

I got all my ingredients from my parents/suite mate so I understand the difficulty of getting flour and butter if you don’t typically buy groceries. However, if you do happen to buy groceries or borrow ingredients (like me) this website is perfect for you!
